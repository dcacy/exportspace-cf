Welcome to the Watson Workspace Export Tool!

This application will authenticate you to Workspace, then ask your permission to access your spaces. Click on a space to export the most recent 50 messages and any files it finds in those messages.

NOTE: This is a sample application, and comes with no warranties. It exists just to demonstrate what is possible with Watson Workspace! Please don't use it for any production purposes.
